History

  • Originally developed by Jack Slocum.
  • Started as extension to Yahoo's YUI! framework.
  • Interactive Data Grid
  • Ext JS 1.0 was released two years ago from tomorrow, April 15th 2007.
  • Has adapters for jQuery, Prototype and YUI.
  • Abstracted the implementation of its base library.
  • 2.0 introduced components/containers/layouts
  • Migrating from Ext JS 1.x to Ext JS 2.x involved some major architecture changes.
  • Ext Core (subset of Ext JS)
  • Ext JS 2.x was a solid foundation to build Ext 3.0 on and there are almost no breaking changes.
  • 3.0 Ext Direct, DataWriters, Accessibility (ARIA) and much more.
